Turn your home screen into a journey through space! Starfield is a smooth, lightweight live wallpaper that sends you flying through a beautiful starfield — right on your device.

Features:

- Highly configurable starfield
  - Adjustable star count, size and color
  - Round or square star shape
  - Separate minimum and maximum speed controls for varied motion
- Motion & visual effects
  - Star trails with configurable gradient colors
  - Meteors with adjustable frequency and customizable head/tail colors
  - Nebula clouds that drift slowly behind the stars, with adjustable count, opacity and movement
- Background
  - Custom background color
  - Optional radial gradient that blends from a center color outward
- Screen & sensor interaction
  - Follow home screen swiping with adjustable intensity
  - Auto-restore forward direction when returning to the main screen
  - Gyroscope-based tilt movement with adjustable intensity
  - Battery-aware speed reduction option
- Performance & expert controls
  - Adjustable framerate (FPS) for performance tuning
  - Depth of field setting to control perceived 3D effect
- Usability
  - Color pickers for stars, trails, meteors, nebula and background
  - Persistent settings and a reset-to-defaults option
